PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: November 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Sitara Dynamics collects several types of information to provide and improve our robotic control platform:

Personal Information
  • Username and authentication credentials
  • Contact information (email, phone number)
  • Organization and role information
  • Training certifications and credentials
Technical & Operational Data
  • Robot telemetry (position, battery, temperature, load)
  • Command logs and operation history
  • System performance metrics
  • IP addresses and access logs
  • Browser type and device information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Platform Operation: Authenticate users and enable robot control functionality
  • Safety & Monitoring: Track robot operations and ensure safe usage
  • Performance Optimization: Analyze telemetry to improve robot performance
  • Research & Development: Advance humanoid robotics technology
  • Compliance: Meet legal and regulatory requirements
  • Communication: Send operational updates and security alerts

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption. We implement physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect your information. Robot telemetry data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel who require it for legitimate business purposes.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors assisting with operations
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect safety
  • Research Partners: Anonymized data for academic research (with consent)
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. Operational telemetry may be retained for up to 5 years for research and safety analysis purposes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Object to data processing
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for optional data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@sitaradynamics.com

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. No third-party tracking or advertising cookies are used on this platform.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email to registered users. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance.
